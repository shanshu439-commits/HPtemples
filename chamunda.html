About Temple

Chamunda Devi Temple is dedicated to Goddess Chamunda, a fierce form of Durga. It is located near Dharamshala on the banks of the Baner River. it is one of the most sacred shrines dedicated to Goddess Chamunda, a fierce form of Goddess Durga. Situated on the banks of the Baner River with the majestic Dhauladhar mountains in the background, the temple is both a powerful spiritual center and a scenic pilgrimage destination. Chamunda Devi is worshipped as the goddess who destroys evil forces and protects her devotees from negativity and fear.
