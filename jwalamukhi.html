About Temple

Jwala Ji Temple is famous for its eternal flame (Akhand Jyoti) which burns naturally from rock fissures. It is one of the most sacred Shakti Peethas.

Location

Jwalamukhi, Kangra District

Special Attraction

The natural flame that never extinguishes.
