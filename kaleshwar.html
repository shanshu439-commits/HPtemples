About Temple

Kalinath Kaleshwar Mahadev Temple is an ancient and revered Shiva temple situated on the banks of the Beas River near Garli and Paragpur in the Kangra district of Himachal Pradesh. The temple is believed to be over 400 years old and is a blend of mythology, history, and spiritual practice. The temple is linked to divine events from the Satyuga era, when gods prayed to Lord Shiva to control the chaos caused by demons. Shiva is believed to have manifested his powerful form Kaleshwar Mahadev here to pacify dissatisfied aspects of the divine feminine energy.

Famous For

Its ancient self-manifested Shivling and its peaceful riverside location on the banks of the Beas River.

Best Time to Visit

Maha Shivaratri and the holy month of Shravan.
