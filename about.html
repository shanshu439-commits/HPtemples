Introduction

Kangra is one of the most beautiful and historic districts of Himachal Pradesh. It is famous for its ancient temples, scenic valleys, and spiritual atmosphere.

History

Kangra has a rich history dating back to ancient times. It was ruled by the Katoch dynasty and is known for the famous Kangra Fort, one of the oldest forts in India.

Famous Attractions

    • Bajreshwari Devi Temple
    • Jwala Ji Temple
    • Chamunda Devi Temple
    • Masroor Rock Cut Temple
    • Kangra Fort
    • Kunal Pathri Temple
    • Shri Kalinath Kaleshwar Mahadev Temple

    Why Visit Kangra?

    Kangra offers a perfect mix of spirituality, history, and natural beauty. The snow-capped Dhauladhar mountains and green valleys make it a peaceful and attractive destination for tourists.
